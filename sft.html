How good is space-for-time substitution in predicting plant phenology

Yi Liu, Yiluan Song, Kai Zhu
Yi Liu is a 2022 cohort Ph.D. student.
Manuscript in Preparation

Highlights

  • We developed a method to quantify the differences in the sensitivity of plant phenology to temperature over space and over time, taking the uncertainty of both estimates into consideration.
  • We compared spatial and temporal sensitivities in multiple wind-pollinated species to evaluate the use of space-for-time substitution in forecasting their phenology over time.
